# Stopping Physical Production

Sony has announced that it's going to stop producing discs for new games starting in January 2028.

Not that it really matters because most games sold physically on modern consoles do not have any useful data on them, more like physical keys for content that must be downloaded from the Internet.

Interesting that the big selling point of the Playstation 4 was its perserverence of physical media and the simple ability to share games with friends, something that Microsoft was interested in removing during the Xbox One launch.

Video game industries want to prevent anyone from re-selling games they paid for and force the game console to always be online to verify them.
