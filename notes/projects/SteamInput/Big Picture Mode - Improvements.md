# Layers

## Default
- Mouse on right pad
- Left mouse click on right trigger & right pad click.
- Scroll wheel on left pad
- Directional pad on left pad click
- Grips minimize Big Picture Mode, long press to exit

## Web Browser
- Added scroll up and down to joystick

## Chat
- Click on friends with single press of right trigger
- Open keyboard with joystick click

## Select Mouse Position
- Decreased mouse sensitivity
