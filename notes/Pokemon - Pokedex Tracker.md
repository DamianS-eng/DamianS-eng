The way extra Pkmn from Gen 1 will be acquired without trades is via extensive use of the [Mew glitch](https://bulbapedia.bulbagardenjj.net/wiki/Mew_glitch).
Some setup needed:
- Have access to Cerulean City and Fushia City
- Have a Pkmn with Dig/Teleport/Fly, Fly recommended
- Have a Pkmn with Strength
- Have a Pkmn w/ desired in lead with 
- Have an Atk lowering move like Growl is optional to lower or max the executed's Lv.
	- In case of Missingno, keep in Slot 6 an item which is requested to be increased in quantity.

The steps to replicate in the Virtual Console version of Pokemon: Red/Blue are below:
1. Go to Cerulean, walk up to the top-right of the patch of grass left of Nugget Bridge. Do not enter and save.
2. Walk down one tile, and immediately pause to TP away, ideally to Fushia City.
3. Start menu is disabled, go to the PC in Fushia and Change Box to save game, then Reset.
4. Start menu works again. Go to the old man w/ Goldeen Teeth house, use Strength on the boulder.
5. Go into an encounter w/ a wild. Ideally, find a Ditto and have it copy the lead.
6. TP/Fly to Cerulean City, and walk up Nugget Bridge.
7. The start menu should open automatically, close to start a battle.
