<h1><p align="center">Banana / Apple Cake</p></h1>
Tags: [[library/Recipes/]]
## Ingredients

- 2 Bananas
- 1 Apple
- 1/2 cup Coconut Oil
- 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
- 1 cup Flour
- 1 tablespoon Balius Powder
- 1 teaspoon Nut Meg
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract 
- 2 tablespoons Brown Sugar

## Bake

Combine and bake at 350° - 375° for 45 minutes.
